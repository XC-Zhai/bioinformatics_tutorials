Aylward lab Bioinformatics tutorials and SOPs


These are a series of introductory bionformatics tutorials I have developed, primarily for teaching Systems Biology classes at Virginia Tech and also for archiving smaller protocols used in my lab group.

The "chapters" or tutorials below are intended for use at the introductory level (i.e., starting with little or no bioinformatics knowledge). They presume a Unix or Unix-like environment (primarily Ubuntu or CentOS).
